26 LC 112 4293
House Resolution 21EX
By: Representatives Willis of the 55th, Reese of the 140th, and Jackson of the 68th
A RESOLUTION
1 Honoring the life and memory of Sunnah Muhammad Pasha; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, the State of Georgia mourns the loss of one of its most distinguished citizens
3 with the passing of Sunnah Muhammad Pasha on June 5, 2026; and
4 WHEREAS, Sunnah Muhammad Pasha was born on December 24, 1974, in Cincinnati,
5 Ohio, a beloved son of Aldean and Daoud Pasha; and
6 WHEREAS, he received his early education at Sprayberry High School, graduating as part
7 of the Class of 1993, and he matriculated at Alabama A&M University, where he received
8 an internship with IBM Consulting Services and became a member of the Alpha Phi Alpha
9 Fraternity, Inc., Delta Gamma Chapter; and
10 WHEREAS, Sunnah Muhammad Pasha enjoyed a highly successful career in Big 5
11 Management Consulting, during which he rose to the pinnacle of corporate leadership and
12 served for decades as a partner with Deloitte Consulting and EY; and
13 WHEREAS, in addition to his corporate achievements, he proudly followed in his father's
14 footsteps and built a substantial real estate portfolio designed to endure for generations; and

15 WHEREAS, Sunnah Muhammad Pasha was united in love and marriage to his wife, Tara,
16 and was blessed with two remarkable daughters, Sunnai and Summer; and
17 WHEREAS, he gave inspiration to many through his high ideals, morals, and deep concern
18 for his fellow citizens, and the devotion, patience, and understanding he demonstrated to his
19 family and friends were admired by others; and
20 WHEREAS, he was a person of magnanimous strengths with an unimpeachable reputation
21 for integrity, intelligence, fairness, and kindness, and by the example he made of his life, he
22 made this world a better place in which to live; and
23 WHEREAS, a compassionate and generous man, Sunnah Muhammad Pasha will long be
24 remembered for his love of family, friendship, and community and will be missed by all who
25 had the great fortune of knowing him.
26 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
27 the members of this body join in honoring the life and memory of Sunnah Muhammad Pasha
28 and express their deepest and most sincere regret at his passing.
29 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
30 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
31 family of Sunnah Muhammad Pasha.

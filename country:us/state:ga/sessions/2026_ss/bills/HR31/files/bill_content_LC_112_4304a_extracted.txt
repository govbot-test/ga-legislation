26 LC 112 4304
House Resolution 31EX
By: Representatives Adeyina of the 110th, Beckles of the 96th, Dawson of the 65th, Schofield
of the 63rd, Burnough of the 77th, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and honoring the Haiti National Football Team for qualifying for and competing
2 in the 2026 FIFA World Cup; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the people of the State of Georgia join millions of supporters around the world
4 in celebrating the remarkable achievement of the Haiti National Football Team, known
5 affectionately as Les Grenadiers, in qualifying for the 2026 FIFA World Cup, marking Haiti's
6 first appearance in the tournament since 1974; and
7 WHEREAS, Haiti's qualification ended a 52-year absence from the world's most prestigious
8 sporting event and stands as a testament to the resilience, determination, and excellence of
9 Haitian athletes and coaches; and
10 WHEREAS, under the leadership of Head Coach Sébastien Migné, Haiti earned its place
11 among the world's elite football nations and has inspired Haitians and supporters throughout
12 the State of Georgia, the United States, and the global Haitian diaspora; and
13 WHEREAS, the State of Georgia is home to one of the largest Haitian-American
14 communities in the southeastern United States, whose cultural, economic, educational, civic,
15 and entrepreneurial contributions enrich the fabric of our state every day; and

16 WHEREAS, the Georgia Haitian-American Chamber of Commerce Inc. (GAHCCI),
17 representing Haitian-American businesses, professionals, entrepreneurs, and community
18 leaders throughout the State of Georgia, has been a steadfast advocate for the advancement,

23 extraordinary national pride and international recognition, reflecting the resilience,
24 excellence, and enduring spirit of the Haitian people and serving as an inspiration to
25 Haitian-Americans across Georgia and the nation; and

27 hope, unity, perseverance, and national pride during a historic moment for the Haitian
28 people; and

38 WHEREAS, the coaching and technical staff, led by Head Coach Sébastien Migné, have
39 demonstrated exceptional leadership, discipline, and commitment in preparing and guiding
40 the Haiti National Football Team to compete on the world stage and have played an integral
41 role in this historic achievement; and
42 WHEREAS, these players, coaches, and support personnel have become ambassadors for
43 Haiti and role models for future generations of athletes throughout Georgia, the United
44 States, Haiti, and the broader Caribbean community; and
45 WHEREAS, the State of Georgia proudly recognizes the dedication, sacrifice, and
46 achievement demonstrated by the Haiti National Football Team as they represent their nation
47 on the global stage and inspire people around the world through their perseverance and
48 sportsmanship.
49 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
50 the members of this body recognize and honor the Haiti National Football Team, Head
51 Coach Sébastien Migné, the coaching staff, and all players for their historic qualification for
52 and participation in the 2026 FIFA World Cup.
53 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
54 and directed to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to the
55 public and the press.

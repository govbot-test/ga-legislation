26 LC 112 4321
House Resolution 49EX
By: Representatives Bell of the 75th, McQueen of the 61st, Cannon of the 58th, Holland of the
54th, and Myles of the 126th
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending B High ATL (BHIGHTV) for its outstanding contributions
2 to hip-hop media, cultural preservation, and community engagement; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, B High ATL (BHIGHTV) stands as one of the Southern region's leading
4 hip-hop media platforms, delivering authentic conversations, exclusive interviews, and
5 cultural insight from the heart of Atlanta, Georgia; and
6 WHEREAS, B High ATL was founded and is hosted by esteemed media personality and
7 educator Brian "B High" Hightower alongside co-host Roderick "Wicked" Barber, whose
8 combined efforts have created a platform that bridges the gap between artists, industry
9 professionals, and the broader community; and
10 WHEREAS, through its commitment to highlighting the real stories behind the music, B
11 High ATL has provided a voice to artists, producers, DJs, and influential figures, capturing
12 the depth, diversity, and evolution of hip-hop culture; and
13 WHEREAS, with a digital reach exceeding 350,000 subscribers and more than 140 million
14 views, BHIGHTV has established itself as a trusted and impactful voice within the
15 entertainment industry, particularly in elevating the Southern hip-hop narrative; and

16 WHEREAS, the platform is widely respected for its raw and unfiltered interview style,
17 preserving the history and documenting the cultural significance of hip-hop while fostering
18 meaningful dialogue within the community; and
19 WHEREAS, beyond entertainment, B High ATL serves as an educational and inspirational
20 resource, offering audiences valuable insight into the business, lifestyle, and enduring legacy
21 of hip-hop culture; and
22 WHEREAS, through consistent content creation, strong industry relationships, and an
23 unwavering commitment to authenticity, BHIGHTV continues to shape conversations, uplift
24 voices, and preserve the stories that define the Culture.
25 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
26 the members of this body recognize and commend B High ATL (BHIGHTV) for its
27 significant contributions to media, culture, and the State of Georgia as well as extend their
28 sincere appreciation for its role in amplifying the voices and stories of the hip-hop
29 community.
30 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
31 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to B
32 High ATL (BHIGHTV).

26 LC 112 4267
House Resolution 8EX
By: Representatives Hilton of the 48th, Rice of the 139th, Horner of the 3rd, Erwin of the 32nd,
Cannon of the 172nd, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending and congratulating Olivia Dover; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Olivia Dover has attained the rank of Eagle Scout, the most distinguished rank
3 conferred by Scouting America; and
4 WHEREAS, a candidate for the most exceptional award in scouting must complete a
5 rigorous program of training and demonstrate her proficiency in selected scouting techniques
6 and the attainment of the highest ideals of scouting; and
7 WHEREAS, only one scout out of 97 ever reaches this lofty level of achievement, and Olivia
8 has ably evidenced her aptitude and competency in mastering the various special skills taught
9 through the Scouts BSA program; and
10 WHEREAS, this hard-working young woman has demonstrated great leadership skills and
11 initiative and has truly exhibited a commitment to the scouting principles of honesty and
12 fairness during her years with the Scouts BSA program; and
13 WHEREAS, a founding member of Troop 651, Olivia designed and led the construction of
14 a stepping stone pathway at the Spalding Corners Swim and Tennis Association, creating a

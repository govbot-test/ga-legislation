26 LC 112 4330
Senate Resolution 13EX
22nd, Brass of the 6th and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Tayvian D. Allen; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Tayvian D. Allen is an exceptional student leader, aspiring legal professional,
3 devout minister, and exemplary public servant who has distinguished himself through his
4 dedication to scholarship, leadership, and service; and
5 WHEREAS, Tayvian D. Allen began his public service career with the Ascension Parish
6 Sheriff's Office Community Outreach Division, where he mentored local youth, coordinated
7 outreach initiatives, supported community engagement programs, and helped promote
8 positive relationships between law enforcement and the citizens they serve; and
9 WHEREAS, he further demonstrated his commitment to effective government through
10 service with Ascension Parish Government, where he assisted the Purchasing Department,
11 maintained operational records, and contributed to collaborative efforts that enhanced
12 organizational efficiency and accountability; and
13 WHEREAS, his passion for public policy and legislative affairs led him to serve with the
14 Georgia Legislative Black Caucus, where he assisted members during legislative sessions,

15 prepared summaries of legislation, facilitated discussions on public policy issues, supported
16 constituent communications, and helped coordinate outreach and educational initiatives; and
17 WHEREAS, Tayvian D. Allen has additionally distinguished himself through service to the
18 Georgia State Senate as chief of staff to Senator Michael Rhett, conducting legislative
19 research, assisting with policy development, drafting speeches and official communications,
20 coordinating stakeholder engagement, managing office operations, and supporting the
21 legislative process from bill development through final consideration; and
22 WHEREAS, in addition to his professional service, he has remained actively engaged in
23 civic and community organizations, including the Donaldsonville Rotary Club and the
24 National Society of Collegiate Scholars; and
25 WHEREAS, through his outstanding record of academic achievement, public service,
26 faith-based leadership, and dedication to the pursuit of justice and civic engagement, Tayvian
27 D. Allen has established himself as a young leader of great promise who is befitting of
28 formal recogition.
29 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
30 recognize and commend Tayvian D. Allen for his outstanding achievements, exemplary
31 leadership, and dedicated service as well as extend sincere best wishes for continued success
32 in all his future endeavors.
33 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
34 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Tayvian D. Allen.

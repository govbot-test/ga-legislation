26 LC 112 4295
House Resolution 25EX
By: Representatives Reeves of the 99th, Lumsden of the 12th, New of the 40th, Persinger of
the 119th, and Barrett of the 24th
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending World Financial Group; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, World Financial Group was founded in 2001 with a mission to help individuals
3 and families create the life they want to live while protecting their loved ones and planning
4 for their financial future; and
5 WHEREAS, over the past 25 years, World Financial Group has provided access to insurance,
6 retirement, savings, and wealth-building strategies for more than 7 million American
7 families; and
8 WHEREAS, through its network of more than 95,000 licensed independent agents, World
9 Financial Group has expanded its reach across the United States and Canada, becoming the
10 fastest-growing financial services company in North America; and
11 WHEREAS, many of these licensed professionals live and work in Georgia, where they
12 assist individuals, families, and business owners in evaluating their financial goals and
13 preparing for the future; and

14 WHEREAS, World Financial Group reflects the rich diversity of the communities it serves,
15 with its licensed professionals representing 75 languages and encompassing a broad range
16 of cultural, professional, and personal backgrounds; and
17 WHEREAS, through its commitment to education, opportunity, and financial empowerment,
18 World Financial Group has made a meaningful contribution to the economic well-being of
19 families in Georgia and throughout North America; and
20 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding achievements and
21 positive impact of World Financial Group be appropriately honored.
22 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
23 the members of this body recognize and commend World Financial Group for its
24 commitment to financial education, literacy, and empowerment as well as extend their best
25 wishes for continued success.
26 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
27 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
28 World Financial Group.

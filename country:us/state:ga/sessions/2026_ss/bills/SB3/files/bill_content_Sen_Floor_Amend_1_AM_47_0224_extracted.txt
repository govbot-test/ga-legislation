Section 1:
26 Sen Floor Amend 1 AM 47 0224
LOST
1 Amend the House substitute to SB 3EX (LC 47 4417S) by replacing lines 1 through 112 with
2 the following:
3 To amend Chapter 2 of Title 21 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to
4 elections and primaries generally, so as to establish the Election Equipment Specifications
5 and Standards Committee to establish the specifications, standards, and requirements for a
6 new uniform system of election equipment; to provide for membership and duties; to provide
7 for advisement; to provide for reporting; to provide for the extent to which the Secretary of
8 State is authorized to provide for the selection, certification, or procurement of the new
9 uniform system of election equipment; to declare certain actions void and ultra vires; to
10 provide for automatic repeal; to revise provisions related to selected contests subject to
11 risk-limiting audits; to amend an Act relating to elections and primaries approved
12 May 6, 2024 (Ga. L. 2024, p. 1028), SB 189, so as to revise an effective date; to provide for
13 related matters; to provide for an effective date; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other
14 purposes.
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
16 SECTION 1.
17 Chapter 2 of Title 21 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to elections and
18 primaries generally, is amended by adding a new Code section to read as follows:
- 1 -
26 Sen Floor Amend 1 AM 47 0224
19 "21-2-301.
20 (a) There is created the Election Equipment Specifications and Standards Committee that
21 shall establish the specifications, standards, and requirements for a new uniform system of
22 election equipment. Such committee shall be composed the following:
23 (1) Three electors of the state to be appointed by the Governor;
24 (2) Three members of the Senate to be appointed by the Senate Committee on
25 Assignments, one of whom shall be designated as cochairperson; and
26 (3) Three members of the House of Representatives to be appointed by the Speaker of
27 the House of Representatives, one of whom shall be designated as cochairperson.
28 (b) The committee may seek advisement from, but shall not be limited to:
29 (1) The elections director of the Secretary of State's office, or his or her designee;
30 (2) The executive director of the State Election Board;
31 (3) The Georgia Association of Voter Registration and Election Officials;
32 (4) Cybersecurity experts; and
33 (5) Accessibility or disability experts.
34 (c) By January 31, 2027, the committee shall prepare a report which shall provide its
35 recommendations for specifications, standards, and requirements for a new uniform system
36 of election equipment. Such report shall be of sufficient detail so as to allow legislative
37 and executive policymakers to develop appropriations, procurement, and implementation
38 plans to provide for the use of such new uniform system of election equipment in the 2028
39 election cycle. The report shall be delivered to the Governor, the Lieutenant Governor, the
40 Speaker of the House of Representatives, the Secretary of State, and the State Election
41 Board.
42 (d) Notwithstanding any other law to the contrary, except as provided for in paragraph (1)
43 of subsection (b) of this Code section, the Secretary of State in office on the effective date
44 of this Code section shall have no role or authority in the selection, certification, or
45 procurement of such new uniform system, including, but not limited to, the preparation of
- 2 -
26 Sen Floor Amend 1 AM 47 0224
46 any requests for proposals, requests for qualifications, or other solicitations to potential
47 vendors of election equipment to be used as a part of such uniform system. Any and all
48 actions taken by the Secretary of State in office on the effective date of this Code section
49 related to the selection, certification, or procurement of a new uniform system of election
50 equipment shall be void and ultra vires.
51 (e) This Code section shall stand repealed on July 1, 2029."
52 SECTION 2.
53 Said chapter is further amended in Code Section 21-2-498, relating to precertification
54 tabulation audits and risk-limiting audits, by revising paragraph (4) of subsection (a) as
55 follows:
56 "(4) 'Selected contests' means:
57 (A) The contest at the top of a ballot; and
58 (B) If they are on the ballot, each of the following races: presidential, United States
59 Senator, United States Representative, Governor, Lieutenant Governor, Secretary of
60 State, and Attorney General, provided that such selected contest is not the race at the
61 top of the ballot; and
62 (C) If the following races are on a ballot, one contest from the following races as
63 selected pursuant to subsection (d) of this Code section: United States Senate,
64 Governor, Lieutenant Governor, Secretary of State, Attorney General, State School
65 Superintendent, Commissioner of Insurance, Commissioner of Agriculture,
66 Commissioner of Labor, Supreme Court Justice, Judge of the Court of Appeals, or
67 Public Service Commissioner, provided that such selected contest is not the race at the
68 top of the ballot."
- 3 -
26 Sen Floor Amend 1 AM 47 0224
69 SECTION 3.
70 An Act relating to elections and primaries approved May 6, 2024 (Ga. L. 2024, p. 1028),
71 SB 189, is amended by revising subsection (d) of Section 13 as follows:
72 "(d) Section 7 of this Act shall become effective on January 1, 2028."
73 SECTION 4.
74 This Act shall become effective upon its approval by the Governor or upon its becoming law
75 without such approval.
76 SECTION 5.
77 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.

2 other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the State of Georgia mourns the loss of one of its most distinguished citizens
4 with the passing of Thomas Campbell Leslie, PE, on March 16, 2026, from complications

6 WHEREAS, born on July 13, 1945, to Jean Campbell Leslie and Dr. John T. Leslie, Thomas
7 Campbell Leslie was raised in Avondale Estates, Georgia, where he excelled both
8 academically and athletically, serving as the starting quarterback for the Avondale Blue
9 Devils; and

11 Technology, earning a Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering, and he later obtained a
12 Master of Science in Civil Engineering from Stanford University following service in the
13 United States Public Health Service during the Vietnam War era; and
14 WHEREAS, throughout a distinguished professional career spanning a decade, he
15 contributed significantly to the advancement of engineering and public service, working in

16 solid waste research and planning for the Atlanta Regional Commission, where he met his
17 beloved wife, Mary Head Leslie; and
18 WHEREAS, he further distinguished himself through his leadership at the engineering firm
19 Jordan, Jones and Goulding and later as Executive Director of the Georgia Chapter of the

26 LC 112 4302
House Resolution 30EX
of the 63rd, Burnough of the 77th, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending the Honorable Sylvester Adinam Mensah for his distinguished
2 leadership, public service, and contributions to economic development and international
3 trade; and for other purposes.
4 WHEREAS, the Honorable Sylvester Adinam Mensah is an accomplished finance
5 professional, former senior banker, legislator, educator, and public sector executive whose
6 career has been marked by exemplary leadership and dedication to public service; and
7 WHEREAS, he currently serves as Chief Executive Officer of the Ghana Export-Import
8 Bank (Ghana EXIM Bank), where he provides strategic leadership in advancing Ghana's
9 economic growth through export development, industrialization, and business financing
10 initiatives; and
11 WHEREAS, under his leadership, Ghana EXIM Bank has strengthened opportunities for
12 trade, investment, and economic cooperation, including fostering business relationships
13 between the Republic of Ghana and the State of Georgia that promote mutual prosperity and
14 commercial exchange; and
H. R. 30EX
- 1 -
26 LC 112 4302
15 WHEREAS, Hon. Mensah previously served as a Member of Parliament for the La
16 Dadekotopon Constituency and as Chief Executive of the National Health Insurance
17 Authority, where he successfully implemented reforms that improved efficiency and
18 expanded access to healthcare services; and
19 WHEREAS, he has also contributed significantly to higher education and professional
20 development as a lecturer, mentor, and strategic management expert, sharing his knowledge
21 with future leaders and professionals; and
22 WHEREAS, through his commitment to sound governance, economic advancement, and
23 international partnership, Hon. Mensah has distinguished himself as a respected leader whose
24 work has positively impacted the people of Ghana and strengthened economic ties between
25 Ghana and Georgia; and
26 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the accomplishments and service of this
27 distinguished leader be appropriately honored.
28 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
29 the members of this body recognize and commend the Honorable Sylvester Adinam Mensah
30 for his outstanding leadership, distinguished public service, and enduring contributions to
31 finance, governance, education, economic development, and the promotion of trade and
32 investment partnerships between the Republic of Ghana and the State of Georgia.
33 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
34 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
35 Honorable Sylvester Adinam Mensah.
